First master's thesis : assessing the predictive ability of static macroeconomic exchange rates models (Expected release date : January 2021) [Go to Github]

This is the project discussed in Bello, Hu and Le Floch (2021) where we measure the predictive ability of several USD/EUR exchange rate static linear models based on macroeconomic fundamentals. This project will be released in 2021.

Critical Slowing Down and depression (Expected release date : January 2021) [Go to Github]

This is a school project made during health econometrics courses. Following Wichers et al. (2015) study called "Critical Slowing Down as a personalized warning signal for depression", we use clinical data in order to replicate the results of this paper. Critical Slowing Down is well known to have potential applications in financial crisis prediction.
